Welcome to my website!! ^.^

What is this all about?

This website is a project meant for me to develop my skills and time management.

I struggled with completing projects during my own time.

I would start a project but trying to focus on a project for longer than a week was tough.

I didn't know what else to do and maybe this was a waste of time.

Either way, I wanted to still be able to develop my skills while searching for a job.

So, I thought of making this website and called it "Journey."

I planned a little bit and gave myself a deadline which... might be too long if I'm trying to do this professionally.

Maybe I should have put in more hours or planned to be more efficient.

It's tough to say since I don't have any guidance.

I don't even have much experience or knowledge aside from a 4 month course in college.

Regardless, I decided to try and put my time into it and create a more "full featured" website.

Although, I realized that making a website takes a long time. Let alone the features.

I still wanted to try and as such this project for me prioritizes consistency.

Consistency is my biggest struggle.

Short bursts of energetic workflow is my biggest strengths.

It's ingrained in me to have the ability to put forth enormous effort and energy in a short time frame.

Yet when it comes to big projects, especially ones that are long-term, it's easy to burn out.

Then when I burn out, nothing happens for months and I avoid the thing I burn out from.

As such, I wanted to be able to focus on the "Journey."

I wanted to be able to just complete something.

An actual big project that I was willing to commit to.

This website exists as a result of that desire.

As an aside, I will say that I doubt this website will look any good.

It's hard as a developer not to struggle with an inferiority complex but I try to move on anyway.

Welcome to my space!
