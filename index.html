What is this all about?

The Struggle

This website is a project meant for me to develop my skills and time management. I struggled with completing projects during my own time; I would start, but focusing for longer than a week was tough.

I didn't know what else to do, but I wanted to keep developing my skills while searching for a job.

The "Journey"

I planned a little bit and gave myself a deadline. It's tough without guidance and only a 4-month college course, but I decided to put the time in to create a "full featured" website.

I realized quickly: making a website takes a long time. Let alone the features.

Consistency over Intensity

My strength is short bursts of energy, but my struggle is consistency. To avoid the burnout cycle where I disappear for months, I am focusing on the "Journey" itself.

This website exists because I wanted to finally commit to and complete a bigger project than I am used to.
